Aging: probabily the thing you will try to fight back most in your entire life.

But, how?

Should you follow your medic advice? The expert's secret-recipe-for-aging-well? Or maybe your granny coming-from-the-ancestors tricks?
Recent studies proved that aging, and in particular how you age, depends on different factors: genetics, diet, physical and mental activity, and many more. The interplay of these factors is also very important, depicting a complex equilibrium among them.

So, aging well is no more a easy task (was ever been?). You need to carefully keep yourself under control, without ever leaving the path. This is usually a pain. Keeping track of your heating habit, your workouts, and all the rest. Many websites (or Apps!) are available today to record this, some of them give also some advice on the next steps you should take to improve yourself. However, none of them takes aging into consideration. And genetics? How to check that?
This is where H-Aging kicks in! With a easy web interface (and, in the future, a App!) you will gain access to a constantly updated platform, where to record your stats and gain insight in the cutting-edge research that is developing around the matter, as easy to understand personalized advices. Additional pay-as-you-go services will be available later (e.g. genome sequencing, genetic counselling).
